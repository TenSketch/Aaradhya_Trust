Aaradhya

The word Aaradhya means “worshipped”. It is a Sanskrit word inferring the blessing of Lord Ganesh. Aaradhya is a foundation started by family members of the late Shri S.P. Balasubrahmanyam to remember, commemorate and celebrate the life and legacy of the legendary singer.

Though he is not here in this mortal world, his music and songs have created a deep impact on every listener’s heart, enabling them to cherish each emotion through his voice. Such a legend has to be celebrated for eternity.
